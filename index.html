How To Donate?

Unfortunately, due to some restrictions in the work and lack of access to the direct bank, you are not able to Purchase works as official artworks, and you must use the Donate with payment gateway Paypal or Credit Card methods.

Remember, you can get your work by Donating through the link below!

Donate Here!

Use the steps below to donate:

1. Choose International donate

2. Amount of your Donate (If it's $5.99 , you have to donate $6! (Required!)

3. Your name ( You can put your channel name or your name! ) (Required!)

4. Your email (Required!)

5. Your phone number (Ignore it xD) (Optional!)

6. Your donation message (You can put "Donation for thumbnail design" or just a ".") (Required!)

7. Your payment method (It's PayPal or Credit Card!)

8. Show your donate in Live Stream ( Just choose Show my donate in donators list!)

9. Proceed for donate ( After making sure that all your information is complete, you can proceed with donate! )

10. In this section, you must enter your credit card information and click the Pay button!

11. After your donation is completed, you can send a screenshot from the success page and receive your thumbnail!
